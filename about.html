About Triveni Press

Triveni Press is an independent Indian micro-publisher dedicated to reviving literature and knowledge for modern readers. Founded with passion and purpose, we aim to preserve timeless works, promote regional languages, and offer accessible formats for today’s reading and listening culture.

Our Intentions

This press was created with a personal mission — to build something lasting and meaningful under my name, to share my love for classic literature, open knowledge, and to support my family creatively and legally.

We intend to:

  • Publish carefully curated ebooks and audiobooks, especially from public domain sources
  • Translate and adapt well-known works into Hindi and other Indian languages
  • Support LibreVox and open content with clean, reader-friendly editions
  • Build a small but credible publishing identity with no pressure to profit
  • Establish a base to grow future digital education and storytelling projects

Triveni Press is named as a tribute to the strong women in my life — a quiet legacy that I hope will bloom into something beautiful over time.
